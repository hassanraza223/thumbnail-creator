How to Download YouTube Thumbnails — Step by Step

ThumbnailCreator lets you download any YouTube or Vimeo thumbnail in every available resolution — 4K, HD, SD and more — completely free, with no login required. Here is exactly how to use it.

Download a YouTube Thumbnail in 4 Steps

1

Copy the YouTube video URL

Open YouTube in your browser or app, find the video whose thumbnail you want, and copy the full URL from the address bar. Any YouTube URL format works — see the supported formats table below.

2

Paste the URL into ThumbnailCreator

Go to thumbnailcreator.online and paste your copied URL into the input box on the Thumbnail Downloader tab. You do not need to sign up or create an account.

3

Click "Fetch Thumbnail Images"

Click the blue Fetch Thumbnail Images button. Within 1–2 seconds, all available resolutions for that video will appear in a grid below the button.

4

Download or copy your chosen resolution

Each resolution card shows the image, its label (4K, HD, MQ etc.), and two buttons: Download saves the image file directly, and Copy URL copies the direct CDN link to your clipboard for use in other tools.

Available Thumbnail Resolutions

YouTube stores up to 5 thumbnail sizes for each video. We display all of them at once so you can choose the one you need:

LabelResolutionAvailable on

If the 4K / MaxRes size is not available for a video, the card will automatically hide itself and you will only see the sizes YouTube has stored. This is normal — older videos or videos without custom thumbnails may only have up to HD (1280×720).

How to Download a YouTube Shorts Thumbnail

Downloading a thumbnail from a YouTube Short works exactly the same way:

  1. Open the Short on YouTube and copy the URL — it will look like youtube.com/shorts/VIDEO_ID.
  2. Paste it into ThumbnailCreator and click Fetch Thumbnail Images.
  3. Download your preferred resolution.

Note that Shorts thumbnails are typically cropped to a vertical (9:16) ratio inside YouTube's player, but the stored thumbnail file itself is the same horizontal JPG as a standard video. The actual aspect ratio shown will depend on the resolution.

How to Download a Vimeo Thumbnail

Vimeo is also supported. Paste any Vimeo video URL (e.g. vimeo.com/123456789) into the input box and click Fetch. We use Vimeo's public oEmbed API to retrieve the highest available thumbnail URL and display it for download.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ThumbnailCreator free?

Yes — completely free, no account required, no daily limits, and no watermarks on downloads.

Is it legal to download YouTube thumbnails?

Yes. Thumbnails are publicly accessible images. Downloading them for personal use, design research, or reference is legal. Do not reuse someone else's thumbnail commercially or claim it as your own work. See our Disclaimer for full details.

Why is the 4K thumbnail not showing?

YouTube only generates a 4K (maxresdefault) thumbnail if the creator uploaded a custom thumbnail image at a high resolution. Older videos and auto-generated thumbnails are often limited to 1280×720. The card simply hides itself when that size does not exist.

Does ThumbnailCreator store my video links?

No. All thumbnail fetching happens client-side in your browser. We never log or store the URLs you paste. See our Privacy Policy.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the ideal size for a YouTube thumbnail? +
The recommended size for an optimized YouTube thumbnail is 1280x720 pixels (an aspect ratio of 16:9), with a minimum width of 640 pixels. Saving your files in PNG or JPEG format under the 2MB limit guarantees clean loading across search results. Our generator automatically exports at this exact 1280x720 specification.
Do you place watermarks on downscaled designs? +
No. We believe in providing unrestricted resources to rising creators. Every single file generated, adjusted, and exported from our site is 100% free of promotional watermarks or hidden branding layers.
Can I use custom background patterns? +
Yes! The custom canvas background accepts JPEG, PNG, and standard graphic formats. Simply click the "Browse Local File" trigger under the Canvas section to style your canvas.
